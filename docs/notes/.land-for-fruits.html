Strawberries

The average yield is 2000g/m2. As organic tends to yield 80% that of chemical methods, the average organic yield is then assumed to be 1600g/m2. For 350g a day, increased to account for the loses from drying, one needs (350×365) ÷ 1600, or 80m2. Note, this is using polytunnels.

Blackcurrants

The average yield is 600g/m2. As organic tends to yield 80% that of chemical methods, the average organic yield is then assumed to be 480g/m2. For 100g a day, increased to account for the loses from drying, one needs (100×365) ÷ 480, or 76m2.
