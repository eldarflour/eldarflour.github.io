Beef, grass-fed

A kilogram of beef requires around 326m2 to produce. Assuming a ‘harvest’ every 18 months, and a requirement of around 120g a day for the B12, one would need 66kg per harvest, or 21,520m2.

Lamb, grass-fed

A kilogram of lamb requires around 370m2 to produce. Assuming a harvest every 8 months, and a requirement of around 70g a day, one would need 17kg per harvest, or 6300m2.

Pork, factory-farmed

A kilogram of pork requires around 17m2 to produce. Assuming a harvest every 6 months, and a requirement of around 220g a day, one would need 40kg per harvest, or 680m2. Note, this is a very low estimate; it does not assume good conditions for the pigs, it is not organic, nor is it free-range.
