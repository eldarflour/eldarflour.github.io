Wheat

The seeding rate is 75kg/acre, and the organic yield is around 330g/m2, or 1335kg/acre, or 18 times the amount planted. For 700g a day, one needs ((700×365)÷330) + (((700×365)÷18)÷330), or 817m2.

Oats

The seeding rate is 70kg/acre. The average yield for standard oats was 545g/m2 across 2019 and 2020. As the naked varieties have a 25% lower yield than the husked varieties, and organic tends to yield 80% that of chemical methods, the organic yield for naked oats is then assumed to be around 327g/m2, or 1323kg/acre, or 19 times the amount planted. For 600g a day, one needs ((600×365)÷327) + (((600×365)÷19)÷327), or 705m2.
