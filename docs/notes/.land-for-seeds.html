Flax seeds

The seeding rate is 20kg/acre, and the average yield is around 150g/m2. As organic tends to yield 80% that of chemical methods, the average organic yield is then assumed to be 120g/m2, or 486kg/acre, or 24 times the amount planted. For 10g a day, one needs ((10×365)÷120) + (((10×365)÷24)÷120), or 32m2.

Hemp seeds
