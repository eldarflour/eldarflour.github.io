Sweet Potatoes

The average yield is 1500g/m2. As organic tends to yield 80% that of chemical methods, the average organic yield is assumed to be 1200g/m2. For 130g a day, one needs (130×365) ÷ 1200, or 40m2.

Carrots

The average yield is 4428g/m2. The average organic yield is then assumed to be 3542g/m2. For 120g a day, doubled to account for the loses from drying, one needs (240×365) ÷ 3542, or 25m2.
