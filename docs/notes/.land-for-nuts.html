Sunflower seeds

The seeding rate is 10kg/acre, and the average yield is around 224g/m2. As organic tends to yield 80% that of chemical methods, the organic yield is then assumed to be 179g/m2, or 724kg/acre, or 72 times the amount planted. As around 40% is generally lost in the dehulling process, the yield without hulls is then assumed to be 107g/m2. For 30g a day, one needs ((30×365)÷107) + (((30×365)÷72)÷179), or 103m2.
